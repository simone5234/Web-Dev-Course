Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift has been making music since 2004. She's made numerous albums over the years with different genres from country to pop. Her first hit single was "Tim McGraw" on her self-titled debut album that was put out in 2006. She recently broke a record that was held by Elvis Presley for her newest album "1989 (Taylor's Version). The record was broken due to her album being number 1 on the Billboard album chart for 68 weeks total. She also has the most top 10 debuts on the US Hot 100 with a count of 31.

  • She made some of my absolute favorite albums including Lover, 1989, Reputation, Midnights
  • She was born on December 13th, 1989 in Reading, Pennsylvania
  • She is the most-streamed female artist on Spotify as of January 2024 with almost 110 million monthly listeners
  • She's been nominated for 52 grammys and has won 12.
  • During her Speak Now tour in 2011, she donated 6,000 books to the Reading Public Library.
